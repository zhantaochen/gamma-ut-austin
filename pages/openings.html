Openings

We have two PhD student openings. We always welcome highly motivated PhD students to discuss with us for potential research directions and projects.

Students with solid preparation in any of the following areas are encouraged to get in touch:

  • Mechanical Engineering, Materials Science, Physics, Computer Science, Mathematics, or any related fields
  • AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science
  • Numerical Methods, Computational Materials/Physics
  • Optimization, Inverse Problems
  • Bayesian Inference, Uncertainty Quantification, Experimental Design

For prospective student, please also check the admission websites for formal application process:

  • The Mechanical Engineering Graduate Program
  • The Computational Science, Engineering, and Mathematics Graduate Program
  • The Materials Science and Engineering Graduate Program

If you are interested in joining our team, please send an email to zhantao@stanford.edu (or zhantao@utexas.edu) with your CV, research interests, and transcripts (unofficial is fine).
